Cookie Crumb Clicker

For this project, I designed a clicker style game that is time based. The user clicks on a cookie crumb until they collect a certain number of cookies crumbs. The user has the opportunity during the game to purchase various power ups, and after a number of clicks on the cookie crumb, a real time event is triggered. The user must complete the real time event before they are able to continue collecting cookie crumbs. The user's previous scores are persistently stored and compared upon completing the game.
